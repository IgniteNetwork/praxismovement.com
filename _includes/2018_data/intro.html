Action, exercise and practice.

Are you interested in mission? Would you like to learn from experienced practitioners about what mission could look like where you are?

Praxis is a conference that hopes to gather ordinary people interested in mission. Our hope is to envision and encourage increased missional praxis in Ireland through this time of gathering as well as ongoing learning communities. We have two international leaders who have been helping small communities turn theory into action and practise this mission for over twenty years coming to speak who will share alongside Irish leaders.
